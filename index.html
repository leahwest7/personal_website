Hi, I'm Leah West. It's nice to meet you!

I am a rising senior at Dana Hills High School in Dana Point, CA. When I'm not hitting the books, you can find me playing soccer or football, laying on the beach, or playing guitar. I am the leader of the Journey Club, a Christian club on my school campus, and I am part of the HMO, Health and Medical, Program at Dana. This program has given me opportunites to intern at various medical offices and learn more about different medical professions.

Since middle school, I have developed a love of all things STEM, so much so that I delievered a presentation about Women in STEM to my peers (see video in portfolio page). In terms of career aspirations, I have an affinity for medicine and I am currently leaning towards sports medicine or pediatrics. I love the medical field because it allows me to combine my fascination with science and my desire to help people. However, I am also interested in Political Science and Art History. I look forward to exploring these different subjects more in college.

In conclusion, welcome to my website! I hope you enjoy exploring my code, blogs, and other STEM related projects!
